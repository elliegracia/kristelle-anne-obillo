Titan Academy of Special Abilities

She is Shia Sheridan. She has no dream. She does not know what she wants. She has no plans for her life. But because of being accused with a mistake that she did not do, she was forced to go into the place she hated the most, which is the Titan Academy of Special Abilities. It is a school wherein people like her with special abilities would fit in. A school for Elites and never for an outcast and poor like her.

Welcome to Titan Academy! Because it takes more than power to be special.
