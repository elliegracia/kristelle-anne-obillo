Charm Academy: School of Magic

She is Ariela Davis, an ordinary girl with an ordinary life. But because of an unexpected incident, she needed to transfer school in the middle of her senior year. This school is a magic school where her grandma used to teach. This is a story about magic, adventure, fantasy and romance.
Welcome to Charm Academy: School of Magic. Where every charm is power.
