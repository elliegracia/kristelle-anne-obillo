April started writing stories in collehe as a form of stress reliever from her demanding course. Her first story is Charm academy: School of Magic. She has six stories posted on Wattpad, including Titan Academy of Special Abilities, Living with a half blood and The Devil's Trap. Her stories are mostly classified in fantasy/supernatural genre. She enjoys writing and creating different fictional worlds. April also writes stories that deal with life issues like The Jerk is a Ghost which deals with death, depression, and second chances, and Something Spectacular, which deals with family expectations, college pressure, friendship, and what it takes to be brave enough to pursue your dreams.
